## 📌 Tips for Posting on LinkedIn

### ✅ Do's:

1. **Add a professional photo** of yourself or a project screenshot
2. **Post during peak hours** (Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-11am)
3. **Engage with comments** - reply to everyone
4. **Add relevant hashtags** (5-10 is ideal)
5. **Tag relevant people** or organizations (optional)

### ❌ Don'ts:

1. Don't post without any visuals
2. Don't use too many emojis (keep it professional)
3. Don't forget to include the GitHub link
4. Don't skip engaging with commenters
